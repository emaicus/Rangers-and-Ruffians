Rangers and Ruffians

Version 3.0.0 Release Candidate 2

Release Candidate 2

This version of Rangers and Ruffians is a release candidate! That means it's almost ready for showtime. The site will be updated frequently in the coming weeks with new rules and features as the documentation is completed. Check back often for updates! Release candidate 2 includes the following changes:
  1. New Class: The Gunslinger. The gunslinger returns with two skill trees: the Marksman and the Quickdraw. Marksmen take their time to set up a perfect shot, eliminating foes with precision. The Quickdraw supports a run-and-gun playstyle, where desperados challenge their foes to duels and strike before their opponents can move.
  2. New Class: The Ranger. The ranger bursts onto the scene with three unique skill trees: the Beastmaster, Nature's Vanguard, and Ranger's Arsenal. Beastmasters are able to tame a wild creature, making it their companion so that it aids them in their journeys. Nature's Vanguard allows rangers to become masters monster hunters, analyzing their foes to learn their weaknesses. Finally, Ranger's Arsenal allows Rangers to craft powerful infusions which empower their attacks and make them versatile combatants.
  3. New Race: The Automaton. A robotic race, each automaton must find its place in the realms. From going into overdrive to performing maintenance on themselves, the automaton plays like no other race in the game.
  4. New Race: The Pixie. The smallest race ever in Rangers and Ruffians, the Pixie stands at only 6-8". With the ability to fly and to cast simple illusory magic, Pixies must keep themselves out of danger to compensate for their lack of strength.
  5. New Weapons: Firearms. The Handgun, Long Gun, and Scattergun are now available to adventurers. Unlike other weapons, firearms have the potential to jam, but can also be customized with a number of firearm upgrades. Firearms are available to all classes, but function best in the hands of a Gunslinger.
  6. New Items: Potions. Twenty potions have been added to the game, allowing Grand Poohbah's the ability to quickly hand out potions as loot!
