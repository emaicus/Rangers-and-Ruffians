Rangers and Ruffians

About

Rangers and Ruffians is a free to play tabletop roleplaying game. Rangers and Ruffians was designed from the ground up to be easy to pick up, with a simple, approachable ruleset.

Why Rangers and Ruffians?

  • Good for Beginners

    Rangers and Ruffians is a great introduction to the world of tabletop gaming. With a simple to understand set of rules and easily scalable difficulty, R&R was designed with new players in mind. To this end, R&R boasts simple but scalable core mechanics, while removing the complicated bloat that makes other tabletop games daunting for beginners.

  • Cheap to Start

    All core rules necessary to play Rangers and Ruffians are housed on this site. This keeps the cost of entry low, so that you can start playing almost immediately. To play, you will need:

    1. A set of polyhedral dice.
    2. A few friends.
    3. A few notebooks.
    4. A positive attitude!

  • Constant Updates

    Rangers and Ruffians is a passion project, which is constantly receiving new content including new classes, races, rules, and tuning.

Character Creation

Creating a proper character is a vital part of any good tabletop gaming experience. While you are developing your character, be sure to think about who they are as an individual, so that you can better play as them when the game begins. You don't need to write some elaborate backstory (though that can be very fun), but at the very least you should think about:

  • Where did the character come from? What happened to them in the past?
  • Where is the character going/what are their goals and aspirations?
  • What are a few of the character's quirks/what makes them fun for the party to interact with?
We provide two tools to help you create your character. Who your character is as a person, however, is entirely up to you!
